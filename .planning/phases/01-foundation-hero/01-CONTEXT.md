# Phase 1 Context: Foundation & Hero

**Goal**: Scaffold the site with a stunning premium dark design system, hero section, navigation, summary, and SEO.

## Decisions

- **Brand Colors & Accent:** Electric Blue/Teal — associates with modern tech and high-tier engineering.
- **Glassmorphism Depth:** Subtle transparency with sharp borders — keeps text legible on dark backgrounds while looking premium.
- **Navigation Style:** Floating pill — gives a modern 'app-like' feel.
- **Interactions & Hover Effects:** Slight lift (transform: translateY) with a soft glow — dynamic without being distracting.

## Prior Decisions Carried Forward

- Single dark theme (no toggle)
- Static HTML/CSS/JS (no React/framework overhead)
